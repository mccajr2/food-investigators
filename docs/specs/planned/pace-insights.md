## Problem

After enough sessions, the parent needs a clear read on what’s working (likes,
textures, familiarity jumps that went poorly) so they can adjust pace without
losing him to boredom or frustration — while still choosing foods themselves.

## Non-goals (sketch)

- Auto-writing the next session (`suggested-next-session`)
- Clinical scoring instruments
- Push notifications / nagging

## Notes

- Parent-led phase: insights + ignorable tips only.
- Depends on a meaningful amount of `session-history` data.
- Benefits from `session-parent-notes` when that ships (parent context for tips).
